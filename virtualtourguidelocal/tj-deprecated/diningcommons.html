Dining Commons

The spacious, light-filled Marian University Dining Commons is a popular gathering space for students, faculty, staff, and visitors. Situated between two main entrances that face the campus center, a covered outdoor terrace includes tables and seating, making it a great place to dine, study, collaborate, and people-watch in fine weather. Inside, the light-filled space can seat over 500 students in comfortable booths and banquettes along the perimeter, and tall tables with stools and large community dining tables in the center.

This space is available around-the-clock. And while food is not served 24/7, the all-inclusive Dining Commons includes a double-sided brick fireplace, a salad bar island flanked by five different food stations, digital menu boards, wood-burning pizza oven, and soft-serve ice cream bar with all the toppings. The Dining Commons, which is open during regular hours throughout the academic year, connects to Clare Hall and the PE Center, including a new fitness facility and a 3,000-seat indoor arena/convocation center.

Hours

During the academic year, hours of operation are:

  • Monday–Thursday: 7:15 a.m. to 8 p.m.
  • Friday: 7:15 a.m. to 7 p.m.
  • Saturday–Sunday: 10 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. and 5 to 7 p.m.

The Dining Commons is closed:

  • When classes are not in session

  • During holidays
