Alumni Hall

Alumni Hall

Alumni Hall is a contemporary, light-filled space that is a popular Marian University gathering place. Outside the east entrance, pause and admire the brick bas-relief nativity scene handcrafted by a team of Nebraskan artists. One of two campus Blue Indy stations is also located near the east entrance. On the north side of Alumni Hall is an outdoor terrace with comfortable Adirondack-style seating.

A spacious outside patio with tables and chairs is located between the west and south entrances. Inside, you can enjoy your favorite Starbucks treat and a spacious, open seating area with two fireplaces. This flexible space is often used for events like Accepted Student Day, Student Orientation, Advising, and Registration (SOAR) sessions, and Welcome Week activities. In the center of the building, there is a Papa John’s Pizza and a campus Grille Works with made-to-order meals, grab-and-go salads and sandwiches, and bottled beverages.

On the south end of Alumni Hall, you’ll find the Marian University Book Store. If you need some cash, an Indiana Members Credit Union ATM is located near the bookstore entrance. Head out the south entrance to enter the library.
