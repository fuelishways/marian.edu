Paul J. Norman Center

The Paul J. Norman Center (NC) is home of the Byrum School of Business and Marianâ€™s unique, experiential approach to earning a business degree. Intentionally designed to facilitate highly collaborative, hands-on teaching and learning, the Norman Center features large, light-filled spaces and the latest in advanced mobile technologies. With expansive glass walls and stairwells, the center houses 14 faculty offices, seven state-of-the-art interactive classrooms, four dedicated collaboration rooms, three conference rooms, a two-story presentation venue with tiered seating for 90 people, and a spacious second-floor board room. On the first floor, a central lobby and gathering area includes comfortable modular seating flanked by casual, small-group meeting spaces and individual study spots.

Connected to the Ruth Lilly Health Education Center and its first-floor food court on the west, the main entrance is located on the south side of the building parallel to Cold Spring Road. Outside the main entrance, which faces the campus center, is a patio offering both individual and small-group seating. The building was named in honor of Paul J. Norman, a Class of 1973 graduate whose generous support made the building possible.
