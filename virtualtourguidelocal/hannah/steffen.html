Steffen Music Center

Steffen Music Center

Home of the university’s Department of Music, the exterior facade of the Steffen Music Center features the first ten notes of the university’s“We Rise and Cheer for You Dear Marian” fight song marked in pneumatic notation, a style most commonly associated with Gregorian chants and Catholic liturgical music. The center includes a large rehearsal hall with 20-foot ceilings, multiple practice rooms, instrument, percussion, and uniform storage rooms, an instrument repair station, classrooms, and faculty offices. Students majoring in instrumental and vocal music have classes in the Steffen Music Center. Members of the Marching Knights, Sacred Choir, and Knight Fusion Show Choir can often be found practicing in the center.
