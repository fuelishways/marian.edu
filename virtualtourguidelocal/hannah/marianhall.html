Marian Hall

Marian Hall

If you are a student in the College of Arts & Sciences, chances are good that you’ll spend a lot of time in Marian Hall. The main entrance for Marian Hall is on the north side of the building, although there are entrances on the west, south, and east sides of the E-shaped building as well. On the west end of the first floor is the 487-seat Marian University Theatre. The central hallway is lined with art that is part of the university’s permanent collection. Many administrative offices are located in Marian Hall, including the registrar, human resources, provost, financial aid, billing, information technology, finance, and facilities.

On the east end of the building is the XXX-seat Bishop Chartrand Memorial Chapel. Located on the second floor are classrooms, faculty offices, small-group study areas, and a gallery space. On the third floor, you’ll find the Help Desk, which is part of the Office of Information Technology. You’ll also find classrooms, faculty offices, and a larger theatre-style classroom on the third floor. On the lower-level of Marian Hall are microbiology labs, chemistry labs, classrooms, faculty offices, and the campus mail room.

Administrative and academic units:

  • Office of the Registrar
  • Office of Human Resources
  • Office of the Provost
  • Office of Financial Aid
  • Office of the Dean, College of Arts and Sciences
  • Billing office
