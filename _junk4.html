We invite you to be a part of what is next at Marian, join the team, Marian is made of bold vision and the courage to venture.

Marian is on a trajectory of success, celebrating record-high enrollment and increased partnerships with alumni and community leaders. Enrollment has grown from 1796 to 3165 and the number of full time faculty has increased steadily from 71 to 149 over the past ten years. Marian has seen remarkable support from the business and philanthropic community and was able to raise a total of $71 million over the past four years, an average of $17.5 million a year.

Why Indianapolis?

Marian University is ranked in the Top 50 in US News & World Report’s listing of Best Midwest Regional Colleges, and the Top 30 of their list of Best Value Colleges in the Midwest Region. The campus is located 10 minutes from downtown Indianapolis, one of America’s most vibrant, safe and affordable cities.

In addition to being home to the Indy 500, professional sports (Colts and Pacers), the NCAA, and a nationally recognized restaurant scene, Indianapolis is considered the “Crossroads of America” because of its central location and proximity to other great cities such as Chicago, Nashville, St. Louis, Cincinnati and Detroit. Indianapolis sustains high rankings in the job market:  Glassdoor ranked Indianapolis 2nd for “Best Cities for Jobs”, 9th on Forbes ranks Indianapolis 9th for America’s Most Affordable Cities, Kiplinger ranked Indianapolis 4th for Starting a Business and TIME ranked Indianapolis 8th as the Best City to Start a New Career.
